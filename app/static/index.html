Project Overview

This is a demonstration project showcasing an intelligent sales assistant that naturally converses with visitors, understands their intent, and automatically qualifies leads. The system is specifically configured to handle inquiries about CRM, ERP, and SalesIQ Chatbot solutions.

Conversational AI

Natural dialogue flow with intent detection and context-aware responses

Smart Scoring

AI analyzes conversations and scores leads 0-100 (Hot/Warm/Cold)

Auto CRM Sync

Qualified leads automatically sync to Zoho CRM in real-time

How It Works

1
Visitor Engages: Customer starts conversation via the chatbot below
2
Intent Detection: AI understands if they're asking about CRM/ERP/SalesIQ
3
Natural Conversation: Bot collects info through engaging dialogue (not forms!)
4
AI Analysis: ChatGPT OSS 120B scores intent, sentiment, urgency, and pain points
5
Auto Sync: Lead appears in Zoho CRM and Agent Dashboard instantly

CRM Integration

All qualified leads automatically sync to Zoho CRM with full traceability.

Auto-Sync Features

  • ✓ Real-time lead creation in Zoho CRM
  • ✓ CRM Lead ID captured and stored
  • ✓ Sync status tracking (Success/Failed)
  • ✓ Error logging for debugging
  • ✓ OAuth 2.0 secure authentication

Dashboard Visibility

  • ✓ CRM sync status badge (Synced/Failed/Pending)
  • ✓ Zoho CRM Lead ID display
  • ✓ Direct link to open lead in CRM
  • ✓ Error messages for failed syncs
  • ✓ Sync timestamp tracking
How it works: When a lead is qualified, the system captures the Zoho CRM response, stores the Lead ID in MongoDB, and displays the sync status in the dashboard. Agents can click "Open in Zoho CRM" to view the full lead record instantly.

Session Management

Sessions automatically reset after 30 minutes of inactivity. Each new visit is tracked separately, and returning visitors get a visit count bonus in their lead score.
